20 September 2003

Founding of the PYD

Democratic Union Party of Syrian Kurdistan

A party for Syrian Kurds

On 20 September 2003, the Democratic Union Party (Partiya Yekîtiya Demokrat — PYD) was founded in Syria, drawing together Kurdish activists ideologically aligned with Abdullah Öcalan and the broader Kurdistan Communities Union (KCK) framework. Following the outbreak of the Syrian civil war, the PYD became the principal Kurdish political force behind the Rojava Revolution and the Autonomous Administration of North and East Syria.
