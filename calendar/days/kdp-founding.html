16 August 1946

Founding of the KDP

Kurdistan Democratic Party (Iraq)

Mustafa Barzani's party

On 16 August 1946, the Kurdistan Democratic Party (KDP / Partiya Demokrat a Kurdistanê) was founded in Iraqi Kurdistan with Mustafa Barzani as its president-in-exile. The KDP grew from the merger of earlier groups and became the longest-running Iraqi Kurdish political party, leading the Iraqi-Kurdish national movement for much of the second half of the 20th century.
