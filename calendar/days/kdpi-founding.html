16 August 1945

Founding of the KDP-Iran (KDPI)

Democratic Party of Iranian Kurdistan

The Mahabad-era party

On 16 August 1945, in Mahabad, the Democratic Party of Iranian Kurdistan (KDPI / Hîzbî Dêmukratî Kurdistanî Êran) was founded with Qazi Muhammad as its president. KDPI was the political backbone of the Republic of Kurdistan declared five months later. It has remained one of the principal Iranian Kurdish parties, with most of its leadership in exile in Europe.
