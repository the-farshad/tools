13 September 2014

Beginning of the Siege of Kobanê

ISIS launches the offensive on the Syrian-Kurdish town

The offensive begins

On 13 September 2014, ISIS forces launched a major offensive on the Syrian-Kurdish town of Kobanê, capturing more than 300 surrounding villages within weeks and pushing tens of thousands of civilians across the border into Turkey. By early October, ISIS controlled parts of the city itself.

The siege lasted 134 days and drew the world's attention to the YPG/YPJ defenders. Kobanê was finally cleared on 26 January 2015.
