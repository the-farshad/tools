12 March 2004

Qamişlo uprising

Syrian Kurdish uprising sparked at a football match

The match in Qamişlo

On 12 March 2004, a brawl at a football match in Qamişlo between fans of the local Kurdish team and visiting Arab fans escalated into a confrontation with security forces. At least 30 Kurds were killed in the days that followed; the unrest spread to Aleppo, Damascus, and other Kurdish-majority towns of northern Syria.

The Qamişlo uprising is widely seen as the first mass mobilization of Syrian Kurds and a precursor of the political organizing that would, eight years later, take shape as the Rojava Revolution.
