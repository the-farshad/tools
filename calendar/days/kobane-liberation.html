26 January 2015

Liberation of Kobanê

End of the 134-day ISIS siege

Kobanê did not fall

After 134 days of street-by-street fighting, on 26 January 2015 Kurdish-led forces (YPG and YPJ) cleared the last ISIS positions in the Syrian-Kurdish town of Kobanê. The defence, supported by coalition airstrikes and reinforcements from Iraqi Peshmerga and Free Syrian Army units, became the first major battlefield defeat of ISIS in Syria.

The siege drew the world's attention to the all-women YPJ units and to the wider Rojava project. Kobanê became, for many, a symbol of resistance.

Kobanê neket. — Kobanê did not fall.
