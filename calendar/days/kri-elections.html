19 May 1992

First Kurdistan Region elections

First parliamentary vote in Iraqi Kurdistan

A parliament in the mountains

On 19 May 1992, under the protection of the no-fly zone, Iraqi Kurds held their first general election. Voters chose 105 members of the Kurdistan National Assembly. KDP and PUK each won roughly half the seats and entered a 50/50 power-sharing arrangement that, despite a civil war later in the decade, eventually settled into the Kurdistan Regional Government as it exists today.
