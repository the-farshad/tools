15 May

Roja Zimanê Kurdî

Kurdish Language Day

The day Hawar appeared

On 15 May 1932 the journal Hawar ("the call") appeared for the first time in Damascus, edited by Celadet Bedirxan. Hawar introduced the Latin-script alphabet for Kurmanji that is still in use today. 15 May is observed as the day to celebrate Kurdish writing, publishing, and reading.

Bi xwendin û nivîsandinê, ziman dimîne. — By reading and writing, a language lives.
