25 September 2017

Independence Referendum

Kurdistan Region of Iraq votes on independence

The vote

On 25 September 2017 the Kurdistan Region of Iraq held a non-binding referendum on independence from Iraq. With turnout above 70%, 92.7% voted Yes. The result was not recognized internationally and was followed by a swift Iraqi federal response that included the loss of Kirkuk in October.
