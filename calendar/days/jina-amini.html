16 September 2022

Death of Jina Mahsa Amini

Sparking the Jin Jiyan Azadî movement

Saqqez to the world

On 16 September 2022, Jina Mahsa Amini, a 22-year-old Kurdish woman from Saqqez, died in custody in Tehran after being detained by Iran's morality police for allegedly violating the country's hijab rules. Her death triggered the largest wave of protests in Iran in decades. The Kurdish slogan Jin, Jiyan, Azadî — "Woman, Life, Freedom" — carried from Kurdish women's movements into a global rallying cry.

Jin, Jiyan, Azadî. — Woman, Life, Freedom.
