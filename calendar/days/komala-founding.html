26 September 1969

Founding of Komala

Society of Revolutionary Toilers of Iranian Kurdistan

An underground beginning

On 26 September 1969, in Tehran, a group of leftist Kurdish students founded Komala (Komalay Şorişgêrî Zehmetkêşanî Kurdistanî Êran — Society of Revolutionary Toilers of Iranian Kurdistan). The organization operated underground for a decade. After the 1979 Iranian revolution, Komala emerged publicly and became one of the principal Iranian Kurdish parties alongside the KDPI, with a marked Marxist-influenced political tradition.
